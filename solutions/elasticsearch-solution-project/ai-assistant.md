# AI Assistant

:::{warning}
:applies_to: {"stack": "ga 9.4"}
The {{obs-ai-assistant}} is deprecated in Elastic Stack 9.4 and later. The [Elastic AI Agent](/explore-analyze/ai-features/elastic-agent-builder.md) is now the default AI chat experience in Kibana. Refer to [Compare Agent Builder and AI Assistant](/explore-analyze/ai-features/ai-chat-experiences/ai-agent-or-ai-assistant.md) for more information.
:::

The {{obs-ai-assistant}} uses generative AI to help you understand, analyze, and interact with your {{es}} and Kibana data. You can ask questions about your data, get contextual insights embedded across the Kibana UI, and receive suggested remediation steps for errors.

For full documentation, refer to the [{{obs-ai-assistant}} documentation](/solutions/observability/ai/observability-ai-assistant.md).
